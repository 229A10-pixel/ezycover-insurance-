📋 Welcome to the Study

Travel Insurance Purchase Decision Experiment

🔬 Study Purpose

This is an academic research study examining travel insurance purchase decisions. Your responses will help us understand consumer behavior in online insurance shopping.

📝 What You'll Do

  • Select travel options (destination & date)
  • View simulated flight results
  • Choose an insurance plan (or opt out)
  • Complete a brief questionnaire

⚠️ Important Notes

⚡ Page Loading: The website uses simulated loading times (1-3 seconds) as part of the experiment design. This is intentional and not a website error.

🔒 Privacy: This is an anonymous study. No personal identifying information is collected. All data is used solely for academic research purposes.

✅ Honesty: Please respond based on your genuine thoughts and preferences. There are no right or wrong answers.

📊 Data Collection

Your interaction data (choices made, time spent, questionnaire responses) will be anonymously collected for research analysis.

Your participation is entirely voluntary
